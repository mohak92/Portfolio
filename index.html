My name is Mohak Tamhane

I’m a seasoned Software Engineer with over six years of experience in crafting dynamic mobile and web applications, as well as backend APIs that power them. My expertise spans Swift, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and Java, all of which I’ve utilized at scale. I’m also proficient in SwiftUI, Objective C, UIKit, RealityKit, Networking, Multithreading, GCD, and asynchronous programming with async/await on macOS, iOS, and visionOS. I’m well-versed in OS concepts like IPC, memory management, scheduling, synchronization, and syscalls. I actively collaborate with multiple engineering teams, product managers, and designers to ensure that our app’s latest UI/UX designs are implemented. I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science, a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering, and am certified as an iOS Developer with the highest honors from Kodeco. Furthermore, I’m a certified visionOS Specialist and enrolled in Apple’s Intelligence bootcamp, where I’m developing AI applications for Apple platforms.

Certifications: AWS certified developer associate, iOS App Development with Swift Specialization, Certified Blockchain Expert v2.
